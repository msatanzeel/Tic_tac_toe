Tic Tac Toe

This is a basic Tic Tac Toe game made by Tanzeel.Below are the instructions that must be followed

  • Game starts with the turn of firs player and alternates
  • Click on the cell to make a move
  • By default first player is assigned as X and second player as O
  • Whenever a player wins the game ends and you can restart it by clicking the restart button
